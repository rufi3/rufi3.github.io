ZAGREB IS OUT OF TUNE
is an outdoor escape room experience played across Zagreb city center. You will find yourself in the beginning of the 20th century, solving an unusual mystery that appeared in the streets of Zagreb, accompanied by various Croatian historical figures.

HOW TO PLAY

DOWNLOAD THE APP
Install the app on your phone. You can have the app installed on multiple phones in your team; the app will follow your joint game progress.

GO TO THE STARTING POINT
Go to the musical pavilion in the center of park Zrinjevac, where the game begins.

ENTER THE CODE
Choose "START THE GAME" in the app and enter the unique code you received after purchasing the game.

LET THE GAME GUIDE YOU
The game will take you to different locations. All you have to do is follow the map provided by the app.

SOLVE THE PUZZLES
In each location, you will get a piece of the story and solve a puzzle. You can use hints if you get stuck.

BE THE HERO
If you reach the end of the game, the citizens of Zagreb will be saved from the unfortunate circumstances that recently appeared in their city.
