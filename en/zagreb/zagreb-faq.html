FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

ZAGREB IS OUT OF TUNE is an outdoor escape-room-style game with puzzles assisted by a mobile app. The game is played by walking around Zagreb city center and solving puzzles.

You will be transported to the past in the beggining of the 20th century. You will come across intriguing historical figures who lived in Zagreb at the time, and together you will conquer a wacky assignment put before you and save the city. Although the story is a figment of our imagination, the characters in it are real, and the locations you will visit were actual parts of their lives.

You can play the game solo or in a group. We recommend a maximum of 5 players in the group, but it is possible to play in a bigger team as well.

You can play the game whenever you want, but we recommend playing during daylight hours for better visibility of your surrounding space.

We believe the game to be a wonderful family activity, but smaller children will need help in walking in the city and solving puzzles. Older children, age 14+, who normally walk around the city by themselves can play the game without adult supervision, but if they are not experienced in escape rooms or other puzzle games, the puzzles could be a bit too challenging.

The game usually takes up to 2 hours. The walking distance from the first to the last location is 2 km, which takes around 25 min of walking time.

You will need a charged phone. If you want to simultaneously follow the game on more than one phone, you will also need an internet connection.

You can get the game here. After the purchase you will get an email with a few instructions. The game starting point is the musical pavilion in park Zrinjevac.

Don't worry, there are no complicated game rules that you're supposed to learn. We will send the few instructions on starting the game in an email after purchasing the game, and you can read a short HOW TO PLAY section in the app.

Each puzzle has hints you can use if needed. If you want to skip a puzzle, that's also possible.
