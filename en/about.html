About us

Peculiar Pond is a place where we bring together all of our games and offer them to the world. We are a family group of creators with a passion for escape rooms, murder mysteries, and board games. We love a good story and puzzles that are blended with the game and have their logical place in it.

FAQ

For the moment, we have an outdoor escape-room-style puzzle game in Zagreb, Croatia, as well as two play-at-home detective puzzle games.

Our outdoor game is played in the streets of Zagreb, where you will follow an unusual story accompanied by Croatian historical figures and solve a series of puzzles. The game is played with an app and it's available in English and Croatian. Learn more about our outdoor puzzle game in Zagreb here.

Our play-at-home detective puzzle games contain materials in an envelope and pages on this website. You will be immersed in a story in which you are a detective solving a crime, as well as the escape room style puzzles you encounter on the way. The games are available only in English. Learn more about our games here.

As with all escape room type games or crime-solving games, our games can be played only once by the same group of people, since you will know all the solutions once you've played. Also, you're supposed to intervene with some of the elements in our games. However, we provide a refill kit in each game, so you can give the game to another group or resell it.

Visit our shop, where you can order the games.

We ship worldwide. Our games come in envelopes to avoid big shipping fees, so feel free to order, regardless of where you are from.
