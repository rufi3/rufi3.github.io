the cd booklet puzzle (evidence 5)

EVIDENCE 13
The drawing on the booklet represents a penguin.

Take a look at the booklet. The song list seems to be connected to another page of the booklet. Which one is it?

The page with nine animals has dashed lines dividing it into nine pieces. What does a dashed line usually indicate?

You should cut the paper with nine animals into nine pieces, each containing an animal.

The song list gives some sort of information about the animals. What kind of information is it?

Position and rotate the pieces following the instructions in the song list. Click here to check the correct order and orientation of the animals.

What suggestion is given on the song list page?

What can you see if you turn over the reassembeled animals page?

The solution to this puzzle is "penguin".

the anonymous letter puzzle (evidence 6)

EVIDENCE 14
Somebody made it clear that Lava should expect something to happen on July 8th.

What song and video are mentioned in the letter? Where could you find this video?

Watch the mentioned video on YouTube. Now read the poem carefully.

Every verse of the poem speaks of a moment in the video. The video consists of parts that have something in common. How can you connect this to the anonymous letter?

What does the red line in the letter remind you of? How is this connected to the parts of the video singled out by the lyrics of the poem?

We are searching for different locations. For example, the verse 'Jump on a train if you think that it will get you in tune' is obviously speaking about the Berlin part of the video. However, it seems that we need a more precise description of the location.

We have to find the specific coordinates of certain locations. The whole part of the number matches the whole part of the coordinates of cities shown in the music video and corresponds to the verses of the poem. The decimal part should be found using the symbols drawn after the decimal separator.

Each symbol represents a number and can be found in each verse of the poem, encircling a single word. Therefore, the number we are searching for equals the order number of the corresponding word in the verse. For example, the coordinates of the location in Berlin are 52.5227, 13.3860.

What can you do with the coordinates once you've found them? Visit the locations, of course!

Go to Google Maps and input the coordinates in the search field. Make sure that the satellite view is checked. What can you make out of the satellite images of all of the locations?

The solution to this puzzle is "July 8th".

Daisy's journal page puzzle (evidence 9)

EVIDENCE 15
Daisy was obviously fangirling Lava.

Read the journal entry carefully. You will need another item to solve this puzzle.

Have you found the birthday card that Daisy writes about in the postscriptum? Daisy seems to like the concept of the card design.

Why would Daisy draw these random drawings in her journal? And how is it connected to the birthday card you found in Lava's belongings?

There are six drawings and six lines of text above them. Each drawing corresponds to one of the lines.

Find the subjects drawn in text lines and fold the paper, repeating the idea of the birthday card. In the first line, fold the paper to form the word 'sun' with the words 'still' and 'fun'. There is something pointing to a part of the solution.

When you fold the paper, the arrows point at the words that form the solution phrase.

The solution to this puzzle is "I want to be like Lava".

the guitar case puzzle (evidence 10)

EVIDENCE 16
The guitar belongs to Lisa Guin.

What do all of the stickers on the guitar case have in common?

All of the images on the stickers have a pointing element. What are they pointing to?

If you draw a straight line in the direction of each pointing element, it leads to a single letter oriented in the same direction. Where should you start?

It seems that the only information we can get from this puzzle is who the owner of the guitar is. Start with the 'property of' sticker and continue until you use all the stickers.

The solution to this puzzle is "Lisa Guin".

the song demo puzzle (evidence 11)

EVIDENCE 17
The date 2 May 2008 must be important to Lava.

Have you searched all the suspects? Have you talked to them about the demo?

Lava made the demo in May. Can you find some information somewhere about this process?

Lava writes about the song in her calendar. Is there something strange about these entries?

It looks as if the entries about the song are not written chronologically. Can you put them in a logical order?

Does the calendar remind of something?

The calendar has the same number of fields as the number of buttons in the midi controller with the programmed demo. How can you connect the calendar entries with the midi controller?

Press the buttons on the midi controller whose positions correspond to the entries about the song in the calendar in chronological order. What do you hear?

The solution to this puzzle is "2nd of May 2008".
